About us

Elite Sports Academy: Growing Excellence Since 2012

Elite Sports Academy was formed in November 2012 by current Directors & Coaches, Steven Whittaker and Richard Chapman. Elite has grown significantly year on year in terms of number of partner Schools and participation levels. Elite have developed a reputation for being one of the most recognizable and successful sports providers in the Country.

Mission Statement

The ultimate goal for Elite is to produce athletes equipped with the skills to continue their development at any level of sport, throughout their career, including within professional sport.

Philosophy

We ensure that our dedicated coaches will personally handle and coach every child that trains in our programmes. We understand that every child has talent & potential, our objective is to nurture that talent to achieve their potential.

Key Points

  • 12+ Years in Being Leading Sports Academy

  • 35+ Locations in the KSA

  • 30k+ Students coached

  • Multiple U.S. Football Scholarships achieved in 2 years
