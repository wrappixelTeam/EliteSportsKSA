Illness & Injury

Elite Sports Academy reserve the right to offer a makeup session to those participants who miss sessions due to illness/injury. A doctor’s note must be provided to redeem any missed sessions. This must be communicated and agreed with the Elite Management. All make up sessions must be consumed within the same Academic year as the illness / injury occurred and cannot be transferred too the new Academic year. If the make up sessions are not attended no refund or further credit note will be made available. For long term illness and or injury issues please contact the team to discuss potential options.

Make up Sessions

The makeup session must be completed within the same Term/Season as your payment.
